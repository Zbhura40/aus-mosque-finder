### What Problem Did We Solve?

**The Old Way:**

---

### What is a Sitemap? (Simple Explanation)

Think of your website like a shopping mall:
- **Your website** = The whole shopping mall
- **Each page** = A different store in the mall
- **Sitemap** = The directory board that shows where every store is located

When Google visits your website, it's like a tourist visiting the mall. The sitemap tells Google:
- "Here are all the stores (pages) in this mall (website)"
- "This store is really important" (priority: 1.0)
- "We update this store weekly" (changefreq: weekly)
- "Come back to check on us regularly"

Without a sitemap, Google has to wander around aimlessly hoping to find all your pages. With a sitemap, Google knows exactly where to go!

## ğŸ“Š Simple Explanations of Technical Terms

### What is a Database?
A super organized filing cabinet that remembers things for you. Like a spreadsheet, but smarter and faster.

### What is Supabase?
A company that stores your database in the cloud (on the internet). Like Google Drive for databases.

### What is an API?
A bridge between two services. Like a waiter who takes your order (your website) to the kitchen (Google's servers) and brings back food (data).

### What is Row Level Security (RLS)?
A bouncer at a club who checks IDs:
- Regular people (anonymous users) can READ the list
- Only VIPs (you with password) can ADD or CHANGE things

### What is Confidence Scoring?
When AI analyzes something, it says "I'm 95% sure" or "I'm only 60% sure". Higher = more confident.

### What is Haversine Distance?
Math formula to calculate how far apart two places are on Earth (accounts for Earth being round, not flat).

### What is an Edge Function?
Code that runs on the internet 24/7, not on your computer. Like having a robot assistant who never sleeps.

### What is TypeScript?
A version of JavaScript (coding language) that catches mistakes before they cause problems. Like spell-check for code.

## ğŸ“š Simple Explanations

### What is Supabase?
Your website's "storage room in the cloud". It's where all your backend stuff lives - like databases, user accounts, and those suburb search tools we just uploaded. Think of it like Google Drive, but for website code instead of documents.

### What are Edge Functions?
Fancy name for "code that runs on the internet, not on your computer."
- Like having a robot assistant working for you 24/7
- They handle the hard work so your website stays fast
- They keep your secret keys safe (users never see them)

### What is CORS?
**CORS = Security Guard**
- Websites have security guards that ask "Who's trying to get in?"
- When our pieces weren't uploaded online, the guard said "I don't know you, go away!"
- Now that they're properly uploaded, the guard says "Welcome in!"

### Why Upload Instead of Keeping Things Local?
**Local = On Your Computer**
- Only works when your computer is on
- Only works on localhost (fake website on your computer)
- Other people can't use it

**Online/Deployed = On Supabase**
- Works 24/7 even when your computer is off
- Works on findmymosque.org (real website)
- Everyone in Australia can use it
- Faster because it's on servers close to users
